India-specific expertise

We know the India-Singapore structure inside out

We work exclusively with international founders and businesses. India is our largest client segment — we understand the documentation your clients need for FEMA compliance.

FEMA documentation
ODI-ready incorporation package

We deliver the Singapore company documents in the exact format required for Form ODI Part I and Form FC-GPR filings. ACRA certificate, shareholder register, and company constitution — certified and apostilled where needed.

Flip structure
Holdco-opco setup for Indian startups

We incorporate the Singapore holdco, set up the correct shareholding structure, and prepare the inter-company agreement template. Your client's Indian CA handles the RBI reporting on the India side.

DTAA position
Tax Residency Certificate (TRC) from IRAS

Once incorporated, we assist clients in applying for a Singapore Tax Residency Certificate from IRAS — needed for claiming DTAA withholding tax benefits on dividends, interest, and royalties paid from India.

LOB compliance
Substance documentation for treaty claims

We help clients meet the Limitation of Benefits clause — S$200,000 Singapore expenditure test, board meeting minutes held in Singapore, and registered office substance — so treaty benefits are defensible.

We do not give Indian tax or FEMA advice

Karman handles only the Singapore side. We never advise on LRS limits, ODI filings, Indian income tax, or transfer pricing — that is your domain. Our partnership model keeps these boundaries clear so there is no overlap or confusion for the client.
