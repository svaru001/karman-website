Filing calendar 101

The Singapore compliance year explained

ACRA - Annual Return + AGM

AGM within 6 months of FYE (non-listed). Annual Return within 7 months of FYE. Filed through Bizfile. Late = composition fee from S$300+ and disqualification risk for directors.

IRAS - ECI + Form C-S

ECI within 3 months of FYE (unless waived). Form C-S (small companies, <S$5M revenue) or Form C by 30 November of the Year of Assessment. The YA is the year after the FYE.

GST - quarterly returns

Most SMEs file GST quarterly. Return due 1 month after the quarter end. Payment due same date. From Nov 2025, newly GST-registered entities must use InvoiceNow for B2B invoicing.

CPF + IR8A

CPF for each citizen/PR employee due by 14th of following month. Annual IR8A income statement due 1 March via AIS (Auto-Inclusion Scheme). Foreign workers: SDL applies but no CPF.
