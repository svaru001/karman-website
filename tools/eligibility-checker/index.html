Singapore incorporation requirements

What you need to incorporate

At least one local director

Every Singapore company must have at least one director who is ordinarily resident in Singapore (citizen, PR, or EP holder). Foreign founders can use our nominee director service.

Minimum age of 18

All directors and shareholders must be at least 18 years old. There is no upper age limit.

Legal business activity

Your proposed business activity must be lawful in Singapore. Some activities require additional licences (financial services, healthcare, food & beverage).

Singapore registered address

Your company must have a physical Singapore address as its registered office. Karman provides a prestigious CBD registered address.
