What speeds things up

Tips for a fast incorporation

Have documents ready

The biggest delay is usually waiting for documents. Have your passport, proof of address, and proposed company name ready before you start.

Prepare a company name

Have 2–3 name options ready in case your first choice is taken. ACRA checks name availability instantly - we can pre-check before submitting.

Confirm your directors

Ensure all directors are confirmed before submission. Adding directors after incorporation requires an additional filing.

Respond promptly

ACRA occasionally requests additional information. Responding within hours rather than days can cut your timeline significantly.
