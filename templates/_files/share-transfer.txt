USAGE NOTES:
- Stamp duty: 0.2% of consideration or net asset value (whichever higher), payable to IRAS within 14 days via the e-Stamping portal.
- Notice of Transfer must be filed with ACRA via BizFile+ within 14 days.
- Update the Register of Members and the Register of Transfers in your statutory records.
- Cancel the old share certificate and issue a new one to the transferee.
- Check the Constitution and any shareholders' agreement for pre-emption rights before signing.
