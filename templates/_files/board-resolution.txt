USAGE NOTES:
- All directors entitled to vote must sign for the resolution to be effective in writing.
- Keep the original signed resolution in the company's statutory register.
- A scanned copy should be provided to your corporate secretary for record-keeping.
- Provide a certified true copy to banks, counterparties, or auditors when requested.
- This template provides a general framework. For complex matters (M&A, share issuance with side terms, related-party transactions), consult your corporate secretary or legal counsel.
