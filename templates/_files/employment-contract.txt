9.2 The Employee assigns to the Company all such intellectual property and shall execute any further documents required to perfect such assignment.


10. NON-SOLICITATION

For 12 months after termination, the Employee shall not, in Singapore:
    (a) solicit or accept business from any customer of the Company with whom the Employee had material contact in the 12 months before termination;
    (b) solicit or attempt to entice away any employee or contractor of the Company.

USAGE NOTES:
- Singapore's Employment Act requires Key Employment Terms (KETs) be issued in writing within 14 days of joining for employees on contracts of 14+ days.
- For employees earning >S$4,500/month, certain Employment Act provisions (overtime pay, retrenchment) do not automatically apply.
- For EP / S Pass holders: this contract is the binding employment document. The work pass application separately captures salary and role with MOM.
- CPF: only for SC/PR. For foreign employees, ensure your payroll system flags them as exempt.
- Probation: 3 months is standard; 6 months for senior roles.
