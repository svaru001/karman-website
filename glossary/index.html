Singapore business glossary

Plain-English definitions of every Singapore corporate, tax, immigration, and VCC fund term you'll meet during incorporation, compliance, and growth. Updated for 2026.

A

Governance & Filing

ACRA

Singapore's national regulator for businesses, public accountants, and corporate service providers.

 Governance & Filing

AGM

A yearly shareholders' meeting where the company's financial statements are presented for approval.

 Governance & Filing

Annual Return

An ACRA filing that confirms a company's officers, shareholders, share capital, and registered address.

B

Compliance & AML

Beneficial Owner

The natural person who ultimately owns or controls a company, even through layers of intermediaries.

 Governance & Filing

BizFile+

ACRA's online filing portal for company incorporation, annual returns, and statutory filings.

C

Governance & Filing

Corporate Secretary

A statutory officer appointed within 6 months of incorporation, responsible for the company's compliance filings.

 Tax & GST

Corporate Tax

A flat 17% tax on chargeable income, with partial exemption schemes that reduce effective rates for SMEs.

E

Tax & GST

ECI

An IRAS declaration of a company's estimated taxable income, due 3 months after the FYE.

 Governance & Filing

EGM

Any shareholders' meeting held outside the AGM cycle, called to decide on time-sensitive matters.

 Immigration & Work Passes

EP

Singapore's main work visa for professionals, managers, and executives earning above the qualifying salary threshold.

F

Fund & VCC

Family Office

A private wealth management entity that manages the investments and affairs of one or more wealthy families.

 Tax & GST

Form C-S / Form C

The annual corporate income tax return filed with IRAS, due by 30 November of the following year.

 Tax & GST

FYE

The date a company's financial year closes - all tax and reporting deadlines run from this date.

G

Tax & GST

GST

Singapore's value-added tax (currently 9%), applied to the sale of goods and services.

I

Tax & GST

IRAS

Singapore's tax authority, responsible for assessing and collecting corporate tax, GST, personal income tax, and stamp duty.

K

Compliance & AML

KYC

The process of verifying the identity of customers and beneficial owners before establishing a business relationship.

L

Business Structure

LLP

A partnership where each partner has limited personal liability, used for professional service firms.

N

Compliance & AML

Nominee Director

A Singapore-resident individual appointed to satisfy the local director requirement, without operational involvement.

O

Immigration & Work Passes

ONE Pass

Singapore's most prestigious work pass for top-earning global talent, valid for 5 years and not tied to an employer.

P

Immigration & Work Passes

PEP

A 3-year employer-agnostic pass for high-earning EP holders or overseas professionals.

 Business Structure

Pte Ltd

The most common Singapore company type - a separate legal entity with limited liability for shareholders.

R

Governance & Filing

Registered Address

The official Singapore address where ACRA and IRAS send statutory notices to a company.

S

Immigration & Work Passes

S Pass

A mid-skilled work pass for technical staff, with quota and levy requirements on the employer.

 Fund & VCC

Section 13O

A Singapore tax incentive granting tax exemption to qualifying onshore funds managed by Singapore-based fund managers.

 Fund & VCC

Section 13U

An enhanced Singapore fund tax incentive for larger institutional funds, with higher AUM thresholds and broader eligibility.

 Business Structure

Share Capital

The total value of shares issued to shareholders - Singapore companies have no minimum capital requirement.

 Business Structure

Sole Proprietorship

An unincorporated business owned by a single individual, with no separation between owner and business.

 Governance & Filing

SSIC Code

A 5-digit code that identifies a company's primary business activity at ACRA.

T

Immigration & Work Passes

Tech.Pass

A specialised pass for established tech founders, leaders, and experts entering Singapore's tech ecosystem.

U

Governance & Filing

UEN

A 9 or 10-character identifier issued by ACRA to every Singapore-registered business entity.

V

Fund & VCC

VCC

A flexible fund vehicle introduced by MAS in 2020, designed to host investment funds with variable share capital.
