Where Karman and Osome differ most

Both Karman and Osome serve Singapore-based founders well for standard corporate services. The differences matter most for founders who need VCC services, deep advisory, or extensive free tools for pre-engagement research.

Choose Karman if...

You need VCC fund administration, Employment Pass support, specialist incorporation advisory, or want Singapore-specific expertise beyond accounting. Karman's 17 free tools and advisory depth are not replicated at Osome.

Choose Osome if...

Your primary need is automated accounting and bookkeeping with a strong tech interface. Osome's accounting automation is strong, particularly for ecommerce sellers who need bank reconciliation and sales channel integrations (Shopify, Stripe).

VCC and fund services

Karman offers full VCC fund administration including sub-fund setup, MAS regulatory compliance, and 13O/13U tax incentive support. Osome does not offer VCC services. For any fund or family office structure, Karman is the only choice of the two.

Free tools and resources

Osome offers 6 free tools (name generator, SSIC search, GST calculator). Karman offers 17 tools including EP COMPASS Calculator, Startup Tax Projection, Compliance Health Check, and Founder Pay Optimizer. If you're doing pre-engagement research, Karman's tool suite is significantly deeper.

Frequently asked questions

Is Karman cheaper than Osome?

Pricing is comparable at the entry level. Karman's incorporation starts from S$699, corporate secretary from S$400/year. Osome's pricing varies by package. The best comparison is to check both providers' published pricing and use Karman's free Cost Calculator to model your specific needs.

Does Osome offer VCC fund administration?

No. Osome's core strength is accounting and corporate secretary technology. VCC (Variable Capital Company) fund administration is a specialist service requiring regulatory expertise that Osome does not offer. Karman is one of the few providers with dedicated VCC capabilities, including sub-fund compliance and MAS reporting.

How does Karman's accounting service compare to Osome's?

Osome is primarily an accounting automation platform with corp sec as an add-on. Karman is a full corporate services firm with accounting as one of several services. If accounting automation and Xero/QuickBooks integration with bank feeds is your top priority, Osome is strong. If you need integrated incorporation, corp sec, accounting, tax, and compliance advisory from one team, Karman's all-in-one model is the better fit.

Can I switch from Osome to Karman?

Yes. Switching corporate secretary or accounting providers in Singapore is straightforward - it involves a change of company secretary notification to ACRA and transferring your statutory registers and accounting data. Karman handles the full transition process. There's typically no disruption to your business operations during the switch.
