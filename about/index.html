Credentials

Regulated and recognised

Karman operates under the regulatory framework that Singapore requires of all corporate service providers.

🏛️

ACRA Registered Filing Agent

Karman is an ACRA-approved Registered Filing Agent (RFA) - the mandatory credential for any firm submitting corporate filings on behalf of clients in Singapore. Only RFAs can file directly with BizFile+.

🛡️

MAS-regulated environment

Singapore corporate service providers operate within MAS's AML/CFT framework. Karman conducts full KYC on all clients in accordance with the Corruption, Drug Trafficking and Other Serious Crimes Act.

📍

Singapore-based team

Our entire team is based in Singapore. We do not outsource compliance work offshore. Your filings are prepared and submitted by qualified professionals in the same regulatory environment as your company.

📊

Qualified accountants

Our accounting team holds professional qualifications recognised by ACRA for the preparation of Singapore financial statements - including XBRL-compliant full financial statements for larger companies.

🌐

Global founder experience

We have incorporated Singapore companies for founders from over 40 countries. We understand the nuances of foreign ownership, nominee director arrangements, and multi-jurisdiction corporate structures.

⭐

Trusted track record

Hundreds of Singapore companies managed across tech startups, e-commerce businesses, consulting firms, and fund structures. Every client gets the same level of attention regardless of company size.
